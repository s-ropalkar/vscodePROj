About Us

Founded in 2012, Suswaad Café has been serving delicious, freshly prepared dishes made with the finest ingredients.

Our Story

We bring people together through great food and thoughtful service. Whether dining with family or grabbing a quick bite, we make every visit special.

Our Vision

To be the heartbeat of our community — a cozy haven where every cup inspires connections, creativity, and belonging. We follow sustainable practices and focus on artisanal delights that create cherished memories.

Core Values

  • Quality ingredients & handcrafted preparation
  • Warm hospitality & inclusive environment
  • Community-first mindset
